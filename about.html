About

I'm studying to become full stack software developer in Stockholm Sweden. In my spare time I'm a hiker, traveler and active learner of technology, psychology and economics.

I grew up in a small town called Katrineholm, which is about an hour by train from Stockholm. I still have my family there, and also my friends and my mentor, who has taught me everything I know about industry and business mentality.

After my education as a welder at high school I started working for my dad's old friend at a blastering company. There I learned everything from industry, paint, company management and customer service. After a few years me and my girlfriend decided to change career and moved to Norway where we stayed for three years. In Norway I learnt everything from working in bigger teams and how the supply chain works in different warehouses. After Norway I felt I needed to take a big break from work and try to find what I really want to do with my life. So I decided to fulfill an old dream that I have had for years, which was to hike through the whole of the United Kingdom.

At the end of May 2018 I was off with my big bag, tent and gear and I just started walking. It took me two months and three days to hike through the whole of the United Kingdom and it was the toughest and the most fun thing I have ever done. I learnt extremely much during my trip. How to manage oneself through difficult and lonely times, and to come out stronger and wiser.

After my journey I started to think about what I want to do for my future. Since 2017 I have been permanently interested in blockchain and crypto and decided that is what I want to work with. So that led me to start studying to become a full stack software developer. I've always been interested in technology and electronics so this profession suits me perfect.
