About

I'm studying to become full stack software developer in Stockholm Sweden. On my spare time I'm a hiker, traveler and active student of technology, psychology and economics.

I grew up in a small town called Katrineholm about an hour by train from stockholm. There I still have my family, friends and my mentor, Who has taught me everything in industry and business mentality.

After my education as a welder at high school I started working for my dad's old friend at a blastering company. There I learned everything from industry, paint, company management and customer service. After a few years me and my girlfriend decided to change career and moved to Norway where we stayed for three years. There I learned everything from working in bigger teams and how the supply chain works in different warehouses. After Norway i felt i needed to take a big break from work and try to find what I really want to do with my life. So I decided to fulfill an old dream that I have wanted to do for years it was to go through the whole of the UK.

At the end of May 2018 I was off with my big bag, tent and gear and I just started walkin. It took me about two months and three days to go through the whole of UK and it was the toughest and most fun I've ever done. I learned extremely much during my trip. How to manage oneself through difficult and lonely times, to come out stronger and wiser. After my journey
